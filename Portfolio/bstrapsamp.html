At the core I have a strong financial accounting background with a BS degree in Accounting from Morgan State University. My intial job after graduating was as an Auditor for Watson Rice and Co. CPA's where most assignments were at various govenrment agencies in Washington DC. I had my best experience while working at the US Agency for International Devleopment (USAID) where I was able to work and collaborate with many ethnicities which allowed me to travel around the world.

From there, I followed my partner mentor to a start-up company and quickly mastered the Deltek Accounting System, a government contract project accounting software. That knowledge and understanding of the system then landed me one of my most rewarding employment experiences at the former Price Waterhouse, now PWC, where I worked as a Management Consultant. My experiences at PW and the PW SMM gave me the structure and tools I needed to diversify and step out on my own.


After a few years at PW, I was able to seek out and identify freelance business opportunities requiring the Deltek System expertise. In fact, I consulted at Deltek and was able to expand my clientele to Wang, Maxima Corp, and VITRO

I then decided I wanted to support not only government contactors with Deltek so I picked up on the Great Plains Accounting software and travelled to Fargo North Dakota in the middle of witnter where I went out for my morning run and my moustache I was sporting at the time became frozen due to the sub-zero temperatures.
