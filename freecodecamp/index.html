Ken Katzen

The COACH, The ARTIST!!!
Ken Katzen - Self Portrait

Ken Katzen was the head couch of the Centennial High School Cross Country team from the schools openeing in 1977 to 1980.During his tenure he became the schools first MPSSAA State Cross Country Champion in 1978. Coach Katzen followed up his victory again in 1980 to become the schools first two time state champion coach. My most memorable moments of cach Katzen are the 24 hour cross country marathon dedication to CHS's first beloved principal Angelo J. Fortunato , 6:30 AM morning practices, consequences of actions, goal setting, and sports psychology!!

It wasn't until I was researching the MPSSA Fall Sports Record Book that I found out that my coach has a deep passion for art. When I searched for him on google, his art was at the top of the search results. All I can say is "WOW"!!!
